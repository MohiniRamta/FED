Don't miss it! On November 19 and 20, 2022 the Jaarbeurs Utrecht will once again be completely dedicated to films, series, cosplay, comics, games and entertainment. 45.000 visitors can share their passion during the biggest Comic Con of The Netherlands. Always wanted to meet world famous actors and comic artists or are you a fan of series, movies, esports or cosplay? Whether you're that board game fanatic or prefer to shop for geeky merchandise… Heroes Dutch Comic Con has it all, and more! Be prepared for a great weekend full of entertainment and memorable moments. Share your passions with others or make new friends!!
